The routine

Four little steps, twice a day.

1

Pea-sized amount

A pea-size dab is all it takes, or as your dentist recommends.

2

Brush twice daily

Morning and night, two full minutes each, to build a healthy habit.

3

Supervise under 6

Little ones below six should brush with an adult nearby. Spit, not swallow.

4

Store & seal

Keep below 30°C in a dry place and pop the cap back on tightly.
